<h3>Top picks</h3>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Justin Bieber:</strong> Journals, Purpose, Changes, Justice</li>
  <li><strong>Bruno Mars:</strong> All of his albums! — Doo-Wops & Hooligans (Deluxe), Unorthodox Jukebox, 24K Magic, An Evening with Silk Sonic, The Romantic</li>

<h3>Why these albums?</h3>
